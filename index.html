System Overview

What Is Glitch Pong?

Glitch Pong is a fast 2D arcade battle built on the rules of Pong—then corrupted until the arena itself becomes an opponent. Every rally raises Heat, awakens hazards, changes the ball’s behavior, and pushes both players toward a final confrontation with the system watching them.

Play solo against adaptive AI, share the screen in local multiplayer, or enter online matches. The game fights you. You fight back.

01

Familiar Rules, Hostile Arena

Score by getting the ball past the opposing paddle, but survive glitch walls, power-ups, Heat escalation, gravity, and arena-specific corruption.

02

A Ball With a Personality

The sentient ball remembers misses, favors the losing side, jukes players, grows exhausted during long rallies, and becomes the Eye boss at maximum Heat.

03

Persistent Progression

Earn XP through real player matches, reach Level 50, install combat perks, customize paddle colors, and build a permanent record across every arena.

Core Mechanics

Enter the Arena

⚡
Heat System

Every rally charges the Heat meter. At 100%, the Boss Eye awakens with a full cinematic — the ball freezes, shows a warning, glitches to center, then morphs into the Eye.

👁
The Eye Boss

A surveillance construct that morphs from the ball itself. It tracks your movement, launches six orbital projectiles, and escalates through alarm → active → resolution phases.

⟁
Dash & Juke

Shift + direction to Dash. Juke sends a fake ball trajectory. Teleport repositions instantly. All three have cooldowns. The AI uses them too — and gets sharper as your level rises.

▦
Power-ups

Shield covers your entire wall — not just the paddle. Split launches three identical balls with a randomised real ball. Power Strike sends it back brutally fast. Time them well.

🔥
Clutch Mode

Reach match point and everything accelerates. Ball speed jumps, music breaks, the screen fractures. An equipped Clutch Protocol perk gives you a speed burst exactly when you need it.

◈
1P, 2P & AI Modes

Fight an AI on Easy, Medium, or Hard, go head-to-head locally, or switch on AI vs AI to watch both paddles battle at the same difficulty. Controls are fully remappable in Settings.

⬡
XP & Level Progression

Every hit, score, boss survival, dash, and clutch point earns XP. Level up through 50 tiers. Every third level offers a perk choice from 15 combat modules. Progress persists per browser.

▒
Glitch Wall System

As Heat rises, corrupted data walls flicker into existence — jagged, pixel-distorted, alive. They redirect the ball, shatter on impact, and multiply as the arena infection spreads. Low Heat: 1 wall. Max Heat: 5.

◉
Sentient Ball

The ball has a mind of its own. It builds spite toward the winning player and subtly steers for their weak spots. After 30 rally hits it grows drowsy — Z's float up from it, it wobbles with exhaustion, and a warning fires. It remembers every miss.

🟩
Four Playable Arenas

Classic Grid, Matrix Mainframe, The Construct, and Black-Hole Nexus each have their own identity. The Nexus adds cosmic lensing, heat-driven gravitational anomalies, and micro black holes that twist rallies without hiding the core Pong action.

Boss Encounter

THE SURVEILLANCE EYE

When the Heat meter fills, the ball stops mid-flight. An angry warning flashes above it. Then it glitches violently to center, morphs into the Eye itself, and the red alarm erupts. It tracks every pixel of your movement, launches six orbital projectiles, and enters phase escalation — alarm, active, resolution. It does not forgive mistakes.

Mechanics

How to Play

●
Basic Rally
Hit the ball past your opponent's paddle to score. First to 7 points wins. The ball speeds up as the rally extends. Every hit adds Heat.
⚡
Dash
Hold Shift + direction to burst-move your paddle. Covers ground fast with a cooldown—listen for the recharge chime before using it again.
⟁
Teleport & Juke
Teleport repositions your paddle instantly. Juke sends a fake ball path to mislead your opponent. Both have cooldowns. The AI uses them too—assume nothing.
🔥
Heat & Boss Cinematic
At 100% Heat, the ball freezes mid-flight, shows an angry warning, glitches to center, then morphs into the Boss Eye. The red alarm erupts and six orbital shots follow.
▦
Power-ups
Three types appear mid-match. Shield covers your entire wall. Power Strike launches the ball hard and fast. Split fires three identical balls — the real one is randomised.
⚠
Clutch Mode
At match point, everything accelerates. Ball speed jumps, music degrades, the screen starts to fracture. This is your final test. One slip ends it.
▒
Glitch Walls
Corrupted data walls flicker into the arena as Heat rises. They warn before solidifying, deflect the ball unpredictably, and shatter on ball impact. Up to five walls at max Heat.
⬡
XP, Levels & Perks
Every hit, score, boss survival, and clutch point earns XP. Reach Level 50. Every third level choose one of 15 combat perks. Progress is saved to your browser — each player starts fresh on their own device.
◉
Sentient Ball
The ball develops personality. It spites the winning player, wobbles with exhaustion after 30 hits, and remembers your misses — aiming for the same gap on the next serve.
FIRST TO 7
The match ends when one player reaches 7 points. At match point, Clutch Mode activates—the ball gets faster, the music shifts, and the screen starts to break. Don't choke.
